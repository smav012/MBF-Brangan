    3 relationships

    1. Administered by – Clerks (sometimes acting as Bail Magistrates)

      Clerks handle processing and notifications.

    2. Authorized by – Judges

      Judges revoke bail when conditions are violated.

    3. Enforced by – Prosecutors

      DA’s Office can file motion to revoke bail.

    Timeline/Legislative History

    • M.G.L. c. 276, § 58: Bail Revocations Statue - bail revocations for those who violate any condition of release
    • M.G.L. c. 276, § 87: outlines the process and grounds for bail violations and revocations, including failure to appear in court or noncompliance with bail conditions.
