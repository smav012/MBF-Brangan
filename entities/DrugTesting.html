    Current Legislative Initiatives

    Bill S.982 : An act relative to treatment not imprisonment. A reform aimed at emphasizing treatment over imprisonment, still requires treatment as a condition of probation.

    SD.2483 - Julian Cyr (also mentions Joanne M. Comerford, Jason M. Lewis, John F. Keenan). Most recent in a line of same named bills that builds up harm reduction agencies (previous bills mentioned safe-consumption sites), this bill speaks not of safe-consumption sites but protections for harm reduction agencies/organizations and people who enter them.

    HD.283 (2023-2025) - Joseph D. McKenna (previous version H.2235). Patient on probation with drug testing must take drug test before getting opiod pain medication. Last action 7/1/24 (accompanied study order).
