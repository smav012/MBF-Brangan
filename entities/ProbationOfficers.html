    7 relationships

    1. Enforce - Section 35

      Probation Officers may initiate, coordinate, or assist in the enforcement of Section 35 civil commitment orders, including transporting individuals to treatment facilities and monitoring compliance with court-ordered commitments..

    2. Monitor - Community Justice Support Centers

      Probation Officers supervise individuals in CJSC.

    3. Monitor - Defaults and Warrants (Willful Flight)

      Probation Officers monitor compliance with release conditions and may report violations leading to defaults.

    4. Monitor - GPS

      Probation Officers monitor compliance with GPS conditions and report violations.

    5. Monitor - SCRAM

      Probation Officers track SCRAM compliance and initiate enforcement actions when necessary.

    6. Advise upon - Sec 58/Cash Bail and Personal Recognizance

      Officers provide background reports used in bail decisions.

    7. Advise upon - 58A Dangerousness Hearings

      Officers provide criminal history and background for hearings.
