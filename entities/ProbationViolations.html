    • Current Legislative Initiatives

    HD.1073 (2023-2025) - Daniel J. Hunt (senate version SD.1100 Patricia D. Jehlen). Regards time served after probation violation. Last action 2/29/24 (Bill reported favorably and referred to committe on Senate Ways and Means) or 12/31/24 (no further action taken).

    HD.3234 (2023-2025) - Andres X Vargas (senate version SD2078 Adam Gomez). Phrasing probation as rehabilitative, extends probation if defendant does not complete court ordered substance use treatment program. Last action 6/5/24 (accompanied study order).

    HD.3412 (2023-2025) - Kate Lipper-Garabedian. Extends judicial discretion for probation to take more things into account, refer to notes doc. Senate version of this bill is less expansive. Last action 6/5/24 (accompanied study order).

    Potential Solutions

    California (AB1950-2020), Louisiana (SB139-2017), New York (S4664A-2014), Virginia (HB5148-2020) - eliminated minimum sentences, set caps on probation sentences, awarded “compliance credits” REFORM.

    New York (S1144A-2021) - “Less is More” Act that restricts incarceration from some technical violations.

    Michigan (S1050-2020) - restricts the amount of time a person can be incarcerated for technical violations of probation
