MBF Brangan Project

Relationship type: Oversight

  1. Commissioner of Probation (Edward J Dolan) oversees Community Justice Support Centers

    2. Massachusetts OCC handles administration of CJSC (OCC is a division of the Mass Probation Service which is part of the Office of the Commissioner of Probation)

  2. Commissioner of Probation (Edward J Dolan) oversees Section 35

    3. Probation department may supervise or coordinate Section 35 placements.

  3. Drug Courts oversee Drug Testing

    4. Drug Courts use drug testing results to make decisions.

  4. Parole Board oversee GPS Monitoring

    5. Parole Board may impose GPS monitoring as a condition of parole.

  5. Parole Board oversee SCRAM

    6. Parole Board may impose SCRAM monitoring as a condition of parole.
